About Me

I am a Senior at Cornell University studying Computer and Information Science. After graduation, I will be working as a software engineer at Goldman Sachs. My professional interest is in developing AI and ML for assistive technology. I have experience with robotics, virtual reality, design, machine learning, and backend development. I enjoy thinking about the entire pipeline of development of a new product, from the initial user interviews, to the prototyping/desigining, to the coding and development. My belief is that users are the most important part of product creation, and developing products that not only satisfies user need, but also benefits society at a deeper level is important. Through my work on various projects in college and at different companies, I have gained leadership experience with groups of over 30 people, developed ideas for new social impact projects, and growing small endeavors. Outside of work, I enjoy playing the Veena, a South Indian Classical String instrument, volunteering with children with learning and intellectual disabilities, and travelling!
