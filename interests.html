My Interests

I have been playing the Veena, a South Indian Classical instrument, for over 6 years. I have performed concerts in cities across the USA, including at the prestigious Pfeiffer Hall in Naperville, Illinois. I was also privileged to be one of 30 young musicians selected nationally, and the only veena player, to perform at the Cleveland Tyagaraja Aradhana concert in past years.

Stemming from my interest in Indian Classical music, I joined SPICMACAY, the Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Arts Among Youth, Cornell Chapter. I served as Co-President for the organization for the last two years. We organized concerts by several eminent organizations based in both India and the United States. Our most recent concert, a few weeks ago, featured Ganesh and Kumaresh, an eniment violin duo on tour to celebrate their 50th year of performing. I also led efforts for a Cornell COVID Fundraiser in 2021. Our group also performs Indian Classical Music for various events at Cornell.

I have been volunteer tutoring children with learning and intellectual disabilities for over ten years. Based on my experience, I created a program to tutor children with LD & ID and train volunteer peers.

In my free time, I love to travel across the United States and abroad! I have been to 16 states in the US, and visited the UK, Paris, and India!
